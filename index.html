    Physics-informed Resilient Infrastructure and Structural Modeling Lab

    AI-enabled smart sensing, computer vision, and physics-informed digital twins for safer, more resilient infrastructure — at San Diego State University.

    Explore Research Join the Lab Publications

    Welcome to the PRISM Lab at San Diego State University. We develop AI-enabled smart sensing platforms, generative-AI & computer vision pipelines, and physics-informed digital twins that turn infrastructure data into actionable intelligence for safer, more resilient communities.

    Research Focus

    Structural Health Monitoring

    Autonomous condition assessment for bridges and critical infrastructure.

    Smart Sensing & Edge AI

    Wireless hardware, LoRa communication, and embedded intelligence at the edge.

    Computer Vision & Generative AI

    Drone- and camera-based inspection pipelines for damage detection.

    Scientific ML & PINNs

    Fusing physics, field data, and simulation for trustworthy predictions.

    Digital Twins for Resilience

    Real-time decision support after earthquakes and natural hazards.

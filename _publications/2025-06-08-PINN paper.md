We develop a framework using an unsupervised physics-informed neural network that diagnoses damage in railroad truss bridges without extensive retraining. The workflow supports rapid digital twin updates for large-scale infrastructure, and aloows to integrate multi-modal sensing, inspection imagery, and finite-element models.

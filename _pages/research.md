## Research Areas

<img src="/images/research-overview.png" alt="Research Overview" style="width: 100%; max-width: 900px; margin: 1.5em auto; display: block; border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">

- **Autonomous Structural Health Monitoring** – Wireless smart sensors, edge computing, and machine learning for real-time condition assessment of bridges and critical infrastructure.

- **Smart Sensing and Edge Computing** – Low-power wireless sensor networks with LoRa communication and embedded AI for distributed infrastructure monitoring.

- **Computer Vision and Generative AI** – Drone-based imaging pipelines for automated crack detection, damage quantification, and synthetic data augmentation.

- **Physics-Informed Neural Networks** – Scientific machine learning that fuses physics-based models with data-driven methods for accurate predictions with sparse sensor data.

- **Digital Twins for Resilience** – Real-time platforms integrating sensor data, physics models, and AI to support rapid post-disaster decision-making and structural integrity assessment.
